- Selector page: `phase8-racecourse-management.html`
- Selector script: `phase8-racecourse.js`

## Race-Specific Preparation

Each racecourse is prepared for:

- `2026/事前予想/`
- `2026/結果検証/`
- `2026/OSアップデート/`
- `2026/保存ログ/`
- `2026/レースDB/`
- `2026/AI分析/`

Use `レースDB` for race-specific records and `AI分析` for racecourse-specific AI analysis notes.
